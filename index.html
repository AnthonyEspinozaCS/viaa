About Us

We are acquiring brokerage firms in the real estate industry with the sole purpose of enhancing them with ethics, strengthening the sales division, and fostering a company culture aligned with our values is a strategic move guided by our dedicated board of directors.

Who are we

Our vision is to become the number one leader in the real estate industry. Delivering the best customer service is our primary core value. As an organization, we plan to acquire brokerage firms and improve them with the help of our active board members. "V" stands for value in customer service, "I" stands for the innovation of constant growth and bringing new ideas to the market, and “A” stands for all the commitment, followed by the next “A” representing all the sacrifices to achieve our vision.

The Mission

We will revolutionize the real estate industry by strategically acquiring brokerage firms and enhancing their potential through superior management, continuous growth and a solid ethic code. Our goal is not just to succeed but to lead, setting new benchmarks in value creation and driving the future of real estate with our integrated, dynamic approach.

Our Vision

We envision becoming the unrivaled leader in the real estate industry, heralding a new era of customer service excellence, innovative growth, and steadfast commitment. We aspire to transform the real estate landscape by empowering brokerage firms we acquire, ushering them towards a future marked by sustainable success and industry leadership.

Our Values

  • Customer Centricity We believe in the power of exceptional customer service and its capacity to differentiate us in the marketplace.
  • Integrity We uphold the highest ethical standards, conducting our business honestly and transparently.
  • Expansion Constant growth is our language, focusing on the market and the team.
  • High Standards We believe on asking for the best shows mutual respect.

Geographic Overview

Embracing the diverse American real estate landscape, we spotlight the vibrant state of Arizona, teeming with a promising 45,768 potential acquisition targets. This opportunity-filled Southwest region and the high-density and economically bustling Southeast states of Georgia and Florida amplify the real estate transaction volumes nationwide. Texas' rapidly growing Austin and the surging housing prices in Phoenix-Mesa, Arizona, attest to the Southwest's irresistible allure. Further, California's unparalleled population size and skyrocketing sales prices in the West offer many opportunities and higher commission revenue for brokers and agents. As we pivot our focus to Arizona, it's clear the dynamic American real estate industry, despite regional differences, is bursting with unique strengths and opportunities, painting a positive and promising outlook for those ready to dive into this thriving sector.

Our Board

Tim Murphy

Chairman

Tim Murphy is CEO of APX Operating Company LLC, dba Boomers Parks, under the ownership of Cerberus private equity ($60B AUM). Boomers Parks owns six family entertainment centers and two water parks with locations in California, Florida, and New Jersey...

As CEO of Boomers Parks since 2020, Tim took these eight parks from bankruptcy to profitability in just a year – in the middle of a global pandemic. When Boomers acquired these parks, they were operating at a $10 million loss. Tim transformed the customer experience and added new revenue streams to generate a 180% increase in revenue in 2021 and had a similar trajectory in 2022.

Tim launched his 35+ year career at Walt Disney World and has since served more than 150 entertainment, restaurant, and food & beverage brands across more than 10,000 locations in C-suite and senior positions.

Additionally, Tim is a Board Director with Coney Park & Happy City - Family Entertainment & Amusement Parks, part of The Carlyle Group ($273B AUM), a private equity firm that operates 150+ family entertainment centers and amusement parks in Latin America. Tim has worked with top-tier entertainment and restaurant brands including Disney, Rebounderz Trampoline Parks, Darden Restaurants (Olive Garden, Bahama Breeze, etc.), Red Lobster, Jimmy John’s, Applebee’s, Sonny’s Bar-B-Q, Denny’s, El Pollo Loco, Hardee’s, Golden Corral, and Firehouse Subs.

Tim has overseen more than 35 purchase transactions involving over 1,200 restaurants, stores, and park locations. With extensive experience in buying and selling businesses, handling negotiations with buyers and sellers, and creating strategic partnerships to build strong brands, Tim has facilitated deals ranging from $11 million to $350 million+.

Tim is a member of International Association of Amusement Parks & Attractions (IAAPA), California Attractions and Parks Association (CAPA), Florida Attractions Association (FAA), American Amusement Machine Association (AAMA), National Restaurant Association (NRA), California Restaurant Association (CRA), World Waterpark Association (WWA) and International Franchising Association (IFA). He is a licensed commercial real estate broker in the State of Florida. Tim earned a BS/BA in Accounting from the University of Central Florida and an MBA in Finance from Orlando College.

Nicolas L. Francis

Legal Advisor

Nicolas Francis is a highly accomplished attorney based in San Francisco who practices general corporate and securities law. A skilled professional with solid experience in equity and debt financings, mergers and acquisitions, capital markets transactions, and compliance with Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) reporting, he is passionate about providing insightful legal counsel to public and private companies...

As an associate at Cooley LLP, Francis strives for excellence, offering his clients thorough, innovative, and pragmatic legal advice. His commitment to understanding each client's unique needs and goals, combined with his extensive knowledge and experience, makes him a trusted legal advisor for various corporate matters.

Gary Kiyan

Industry Expert

Gary Edward Kiyan is a seasoned professional in the real estate industry, boasting an impressive career spanning over thirty years. Beginning his journey in property renovation and sales, Gary swiftly realized the potential of the residential real estate market. He launched his career as an active agent in downtown Manhattan, initially focusing on the rental market before progressing into sales...

Over the years, Gary's expertise has contributed significantly to the evolution and development of the Manhattan real estate market. Gary's successful tenure as an agent, combined with his high-level management experience in the public and private sectors, caught the eye of numerous firms. He has managed enterprises, earning a reputation for his dedication, integrity, and ability to grow and expand the companies he affiliates with as a broker or general manager.

Known for his 'roll up your sleeves' and 'can-do' attitude, Gary is committed to delivering exemplary service, whether dealing with clients, property owners, or fellow agents. His dedication to professional growth and improvement has positioned him as a leader in negotiation and training within the industry.

His passion for assisting others and his prowess as a trainer, coach, and mentor have solidified his status as a foremost figure in the Manhattan market. Gary's contributions have extended to serving as the Co-Chairman of the Residential Rental Committee for over twenty-five years. Currently, he co-chairs the Education Committee for the Real Estate Board of New York (REBNY). Gary's expert negotiation skills, commitment to service, and leadership in professional development have enabled him to build a respected practice in the heart of New York City. He founded Perfect Real Estate Solutions, a licensed brokerage providing consulting, coaching, and training services.

Throughout his career, Gary has held significant positions at notable companies, including managerial roles at Feathered Nest/Halstead, Director of Brokerage/Corporate Relocation at D.J. Knight Residential, and Director of Brokerage/General Manager at Anchor Associates, before owning Perfect Real Estate Solutions. Beyond his professional achievements, Gary is an accomplished martial artist and musician, pursuits which he believes bring balance and harmony to his leadership role. At the zenith of professional development programs in NYC real estate, Gary continues to contribute his extensive experience and knowledge through his private initiatives and work with REBNY. His goal is to leverage his New York real estate expertise and vast network to collaborate with potential partners on exciting projects to create a high-quality, industry-leading organization.
